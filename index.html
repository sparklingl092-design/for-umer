Kabhi hamari baaton mein choti choti narazgiyaan aa jati hain, Kabhi alfaaz talkh lag jaate hain, Aur kabhi khamoshi hamare darmiyan ek choti si deewar ban jati hai. Magar jahan mohabbat sachi ho, wahan sab kuch khatam nahi hota. Jab hum dubara baat karte hain, To dil ki mohabbat pehle se bhi zyada roshan aur gehri lagti hai. Main ladayi nahi chahti, Aur aap ko khona mujhe sabse zyada darata hai. Hamari har choti khushi, har hansi, har baat Mere dil ke sabse khoobsurat lamhe hain.
